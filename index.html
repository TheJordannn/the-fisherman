FISHING GUIDE

Controls & Exploration Mechanics

1. Casting the Line

Click and drag anywhere on the screen (or swipe and hold on touchscreens) to pull the rod back. A transparent aiming sector will show the launch cone. Release your input to cast the hook out.

2. Winding & Line Tension

Once an animal bites, reel them in by rhythmically alternating inputs. Press Left (L) and Right (R) controls (or use keys: A / D, Ctrl L / R, or Arrow Left / Right).

Avoid entering the Red Zone to prevent the hook line from snapping. Do not let tension drop to zero, or the animal will escape on a slack line!

3. Deep Sea & Skies

Sinking deeper reveals massive, highly valuable species. Keep an eye out for birds hovering high in the air above, and search thousands of meters deep to find mythical behemoths.

4. Cargo Limits & Ships

Each boat type has a maximum capacity limit. Exceeding this limit strains the hull—your next heavy catch may crack the vessel entirely! Sell your catches at the Marketplace to upgrade gear.
